Laptop Famous Brands

Dell

If you need a PC for work or school and want it to last for as long as possible, Dell's machines are a great choice, Dell has arguably one of the best laptops to ever exist. Nonetheless some of Dell’s budget or business-oriented laptops can be a little lackluster.

HP

HP may not have always had the best laptop reputation, but the latest HP laptops have taken some design cues from their contemporaries and have really raised the bar. Today, HP regularly goes head to head with some of the best laptop manufacturers in the world and is well worth considering.

Lenovo

Lenovo had us convinced when it first released their new-gen laptop lineups, as their reliability and longevity is unmatched by their fellow manufacturers. In recent years, Lenovo has made a push for extra-portable laptops and 2-in-1s without compromising on specs, and the results have been particularly impressive.

Smartphone & Smartwatch Brands

Samsung

Samsung is greatly known all over the world. This brand’s latest technology and features make it the most demanding and trusted brand. One of Samsung's greatest features is allowing their users to have everything available for them at the palm of their hands.

Apple

If there’s one thing that nearly everyone agrees on, it’s that Apple's smart devices are predictably high quality as it is the very first company to ever launch a smartphone and they are still doing a great job keeping up with the competition.

Huawei

Huawei started out with up-to-standard products which were budget-friendly as well, catching everyone's attention then. Nowadays Huawei moved into smartwatches business too, producing superior products to previous ones with higher prices but still worth taking into consideration.
